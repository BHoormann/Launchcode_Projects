Hardware Troubleshooting

Wiring

At the base level, almost every step of troubleshooting starts with wire. Most issues start when one end cannot communicate with the other; and within telecom, that is done through wire. Some data connections require a specific pattern. You can reference this website to see which cables need to go where.

Phone

If there is an issue with speaker cutting out, handset not working, or even a display not working; maybe the issue is the physical phone itself.

Card

If certain ends are still not recieving power or the ports are starting to go out, then there might be an issue with the card itself.

System

One of the final steps in troubleshooting is trying to swap out hardware. Sometimes that even means the entire cabinet. System issues usually come from faulty power issues going into it. Eventually that stress can cause certain parts on the backplane to fail.
